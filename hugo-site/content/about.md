---
I am an international robotics and coding educator with six years of experience delivering online instruction to students aged 6 to 18 across the United States, Australia, Europe, Asia, and the Middle East.

My primary expertise is in Roblox game development using Roblox Studio. I teach students game design, Lua scripting, and 3D environment creation, guiding them to build their own games while strengthening their programming skills and creative thinking. Additionally, I teach block-based coding (MIT Scratch, Code.org), Arduino and LEGO-based robotics, AI fundamentals, Minecraft Education, and web development (HTML, CSS, and JavaScript).

I am also experienced in developing structured curriculum resources, including lesson plans, instructional videos, and project-based learning materials tailored to diverse age groups. I hold a Master's degree in Physics with a specialization in Electronics, which helps me simplify complex technical concepts into engaging, student-centered lessons.
