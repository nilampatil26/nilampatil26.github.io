I am an international robotics and coding educator with more than six years of experience teaching students aged 6 to 18 across the United States, Australia, Europe, Asia, and the Middle East.

My core expertise is in Roblox game development using Roblox Studio, where I help learners explore game design, Lua scripting, and 3D world-building while developing confidence in programming and creative problem-solving. I also teach block-based coding through MIT Scratch and Code.org, Arduino and LEGO robotics, AI fundamentals, Minecraft Education, and web development with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Beyond live instruction, I create structured learning experiences through lesson plans, instructional videos, and project-based curriculum tailored to different age groups and learning styles. I hold a Master's degree in Physics with a specialization in Electronics, and I bring that foundation into every class by turning complex technical ideas into clear, engaging, and student-friendly learning.
